Know Various Features

Screen Sharing

You May Share your screen, application window, present your documents, slides and more. Up to 4k resolution and speed up to 60fps.

WebCam Streaming

Having the webcam on, allows participants to make a deeper connection with you. Up to 4k resolution and speed up to 60fps.

Chat Room

You May Chat with others in the meeting, either public chat or private, save the meeting messages. An integrated emoji picker to show your feeling.

Meeting Recording

You May Record your Screen, WebCam and Audio. Save it for use in the future or to share with others.

Interactive Whiteboard

There is an advanced collaborative whiteboard to draw and explain your concepts to the other participants in the meeting.

File Sharing

You May Share any types of files to all participants in the meeting, in total security, thanks to Datagram Transport Layer Security (DTLS).

Total Privacy

Data stays between you and your participants. KM doesn't collect or share personal information. It's built for privacy first.
